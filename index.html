Hi! I am Baka Bob and I am a novice bonsai grower.

If you are interested in growing your own bonsai at home, I suggest you start with the following:

  • Google beginner bonsai
  • Watch a ton of bonsai videos on YouTube
  • Look up bonsai on Pinterest
    (You can even look at my Bonsai Pin Collection )

When starting out, stick to the basics. Study up on:

  • Good trees for beginners
  • Basic bonsai shapes
  • How to wire bonsai
  • How to prune bonsai
  • Bonsai soil
  • Caring for bonsai

I was happy when I could finally keep a bonsai alive for more that a year!!!

Once you finally learn the basics, you can start to learn about advanced topics like:

  • Nebari
  • Deadwood
  • Ramification
